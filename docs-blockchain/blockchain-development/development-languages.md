# Development Languages

## Development Languages

* Development languages vary from one platform to another
* Many platforms offer multiple choices of development languages
* Some platforms use their own specialized development language&#x20;
  * `Ethereum` & `Solidity`
* Some other platforms re-use existing languages&#x20;
  * `Hyperledger` & `JavaScript`/`Python`/`Go`
* In general, blockchain languages are not overly complex or difficult to learn&#x20;
  * they are relatively new, and have not had time to become overly complex or nuanced&#x20;
