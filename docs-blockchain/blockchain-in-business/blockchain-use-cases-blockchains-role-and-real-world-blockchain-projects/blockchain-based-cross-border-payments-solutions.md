# Blockchain-Based Cross-Border Payments Solutions

## Blockchain-Based Cross-Border Payments Solutions

#### `XRP`: `Ripple`&#x20;

Ripple — blockchain company that provides solutions for cross-border payments

**`RippleNet`** platform aims to facilitate fast and low-cost international transactions for financial institutions using their **`XRP`** cryptocurrency as a bridge currency

{% embed url="https://ripple.com/xrp/" %}

#### `XLM`: `Stellar`&#x20;

blockchain network focused on cross-border payments; enables the issuance and transfer of digital assets, including **fiat-backed tokens**, across borders with the goal of increasing financial inclusion

{% embed url="https://www.stellar.org/" %}

#### `IBM World Wire`:

payment network that aims to simplify cross border transactions and foreign exchange by providing a real-time clearing and settlement system

* supports multiple currencies and payment types

{% embed url="https://www.ibm.com/support/pages/ibm-blockchain-world-wire-revolutionize-cross-border-payments" %}

#### `Veem`:

formerly known as `Align Commerce`

uses blockchain technology to streamline cross-border payments for small and medium-sized businesses

{% embed url="https://www.veem.com/" %}

#### `BitPesa` — `BFX`:

`BitPesa` rebranded as `BFX`, was focused on using blockchain technology facilitate remittances and cross-border payments in Africa and other regions

{% embed url="https://account.bitpesa.co/" %}

#### `TransferWise` / `Wise`:

not entirely blockchain-based

uses a borderless account system that employs blockchain-like technology to ensure efficient and low-cost international money transfers

{% embed url="https://wise.com/" %}

#### `AZA Financ`e (formerly `BitPaya`):

enable faster and cheaper cross-border transactions in Africa, particularly for businesses

{% embed url="https://azafinance.com/" %}

#### `SendFriend`:

remittance platform that focuses on sending money from the United States to the Philippines, aiming to reduce the cost and time of cross-border transactions

{% embed url="https://www.sendfriend.io/" %}
