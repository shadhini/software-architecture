# Blockchain-Based Healthcare Data Sharing Platforms

## Blockchain-Based Healthcare Data Sharing Platforms

#### `MediBloc`:

health information platform that allows patients to securely manage and share their medical data with healthcare providers and researchers

{% embed url="https://medibloc.com/" %}

#### `Medicalchain`:

enables patients to have control over their health records, granting access to healthcare providers as needed while maintaining data privacy

{% embed url="https://www.eu-startups.com/directory/medicalchain/" %}

#### `Hashed Health`:

An organization working to create blockchain solutions for healthcare challenges, including legacy system interoperability, claims processing, and provider credentialing

{% embed url="https://www.hashedhealth.com/" %}

#### `SimplyVital Health`:

aims to enhance data sharing and care coordination among healthcare providers through a blockchain-powered platform

{% embed url="https://www.ledgerinsights.com/science-foundation-simplyvital-hipaa-blockchain-protocol/" %}

#### `Guardtime`:

Collaborating with governments and healthcare providers, `Guardtime` uses blockchain to secure health records, ensuring data integrity and privacy

{% embed url="https://guardtime.com/" %}

#### `Clinicall`:

A mobile app and platform that allows users to manage their medical data securely, facilitating communication & data sharing with healthcare providers

{% embed url="https://www.clinicall.com/" %}

#### `Nebula Genomics`:

uses blockchain to enable individuals to control and monetize their genomic data while maintaining privacy and security

* people can monetize their DNA data&#x20;

{% embed url="https://nebula.org/" %}

#### `Medical Diagnostic Web`:

A platform that uses blockchain to store and share diagnostic imaging data securely, enhancing collaboration among medical professionals&#x20;

{% embed url="https://www.medlg.com/medical-diagnostic-web/" %}
