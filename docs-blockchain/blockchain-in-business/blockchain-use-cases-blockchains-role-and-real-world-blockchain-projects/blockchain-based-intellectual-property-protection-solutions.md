# Blockchain-Based Intellectual Property Protection Solutions

## Blockchain-Based Intellectual Property Protection Solutions

#### `Mycelia`:

founded by musician `Imogen Heap`&#x20;

aims to empower artists and creators by using blockchain technology to establish a transparent and fair system for tracking and managing intellectual property rights&#x20;

* they focus on creating a direct connection between creators and their audiences while ensuring proper compensation

{% embed url="http://myceliaformusic.org/" %}

#### `KodakOne`:

developed by `WENN Digital` in partnership with `Eastman Kodak Company`

* focuses on helping photographers and artists manage and protect their image rights
* the platform uses blockchain to monitor the usage of images and facilitate licensing transactions

{% embed url="https://www.kodak.com/en/company/press-release/blockchain-initiative" %}

#### `VERSES`:

blockchain platform designed to help content creators, including musicians, writers, and artists, manage their intellectual property rights more effectively

* offers tools to create, track, and monetize content while maintaining control over how their works are used

{% embed url="https://www.verses.gg/" %}

#### `Ujo Music`:

aims to empower musicians by using blockchain to enable direct connections between artists and fans

* allows artists to tokenize and sell their music directly to consumers, ensuring proper compensation and control over their creations

{% embed url="https://blog.ujomusic.com/tagged/blockchain" %}
