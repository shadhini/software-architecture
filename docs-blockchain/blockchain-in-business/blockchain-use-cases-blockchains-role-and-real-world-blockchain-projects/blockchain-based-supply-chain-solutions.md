# Blockchain-Based Supply Chain Solutions

## Blockchain-Based Supply Chain Solutions

#### `IBM Food Trust`:&#x20;

aims to provide consumers with information about the origin and journey of their food products

{% embed url="https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/food-trust?topic=overview" %}

#### `Walmart`:&#x20;

aims to quickly identify sources of contamination in the event of food safety recalls — experiments to track the movement of fresh produce from farm to store shelves&#x20;

* has the largest publicly known deployment of blockchain

{% embed url="https://changelly.com/blog/walmart-blockchain/" %}

{% hint style="info" %}
**`Walmart`** has a long and established track record of using emerging technologies and bringing them to market acceptance just by their sheer size and dominance.

e.g:

* **`RFID`**: Radio Frequency ID tags — this technology was once relatively obscure; Walmart's adoption catapulted the technology into mainstream adoption
{% endhint %}

#### `Maersk and IBM's TradeLens`:&#x20;

aims to digitize and streamline the flow of goods, reducing paperwork and improving visibility — platform for global trade

* _Maersk:_ a shipping company, collaborated with IBM&#x20;
* <mark style="background-color:$danger;">⊖</mark> in some cases shipping turnaround times have been increased by 700% or more — due to inherent inefficiency&#x20;

{% embed url="https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/maersk-and-ibm-introduce-tradelens-blockchain-shipping-solution-300694642.html" %}

{% embed url="https://www.maersk.com/news/articles/2022/11/29/maersk-and-ibm-to-discontinue-tradelens" %}

#### `Everledger`:&#x20;

uses blockchain to track the provenance of high-value assets, such as diamonds and fine wines —  helps combat fraud and ensures the authenticity of these assets

{% embed url="https://everledger.io/" %}

#### ﻿﻿`De Beers Group`:&#x20;

provides customers with information about the diamond's origin and ethical sourcing (i.e: source ethically with fair labor practices) — trace the journey of diamonds from mine to retail

* _De Beers_ — a diamond company

[https://www.debeersgroup.com/news-insights/business-market/2022/de-beers-group-introduces-worlds-first-blockchain-backed-diamond-source-platform-at-scale](https://www.debeersgroup.com/news-insights/business-market/2022/de-beers-group-introduces-worlds-first-blockchain-backed-diamond-source-platform-at-scale)&#x20;

#### `Volvo and Circulor`: &#x20;

trace the origin of cobalt used in electric vehicle batteries — to ensure responsible sourcing and minimize the environmental impact

* _Volvo_ cars partnered with blockchain company _Circulor_

#### `Vechain`:&#x20;

offers solutions for tracking products, verifying authenticity, and ensuring quality throughout the vehicle supply chain

{% embed url="https://vechain.org/" %}

#### `Te-Food`:

provides tools for tracking food products from farm to table, improving food safety and quality

{% embed url="https://te-food.com/" %}
