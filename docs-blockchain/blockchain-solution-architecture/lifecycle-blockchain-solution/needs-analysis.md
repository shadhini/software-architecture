#### **1Ô∏è‚É£ Review / Define business objectives**&#x20;

#### 4Ô∏è‚É£ Organization SWOT analysis

SWOT analysis

* what our **strengths** and **weaknesses** are
* what **opportunities** and **threats** exist in the marketplace

it is very important that your stakeholder audience is aligned on these decisions



üìå Consider a workshop session with your stakeholder audience where you focus on 4 questions:

1. What are the **strengths** of your organization?
   1. How can the adoption of blockchain and other emerging technologies help contribute to the existing organizational strengths?&#x20;
   2. Would the adoption of blockchain or emerging technologies create additional strengths?
2. What are your organizational **weaknesses**?
   1. What are you bad at and what can be improved?&#x20;
   2. Can blockchain improve these weaknesses and how?
3. Do any market **opportunities** exist and can they be capitalized upon using blockchain or blockchain and Web3 technologies?
4. What market **threats** exist in the marketplace that could potentially be mitigated or offset by adopting blockchain, Web3, or other emerging technologies?

2. Ensure you and your stakeholder team have arrived at a solid understanding of the **alignment between technology objectives and business objectives**.

   * Who has insight and visibility into this particular process and can give us information and report on its execution?
   * What are the metrics collected for this process and how is it measured?
   * What process changes and updates are required and how are they communicated across the organization and across our larger business network?

### Key Participants in the Needs Analysis Phase

* **Senior Business Leadership**
  * responsibilities:&#x20;
    * setting the direction of the business&#x20;
    * defining, re-defining, and clarifying business objectives
  * task of leadership team:
    * making decisions for the organization and steer the ship as they understand where it's going in the long term
* **Business Analysts**
  * provide valuable insights and supporting details around business objectives that have been defined by the senior leadership team
  * bring understanding of critical **business processes** and how they work together to deliver goods/services to your customers
* **Product / Program Managers**
  * provide expertise and inside knowledge of current product / service offerings
  * provide valuable insight on the impact blockchain technology may or may not have on the customer experience
* **Leadership Representatives**
  * represent leadership at the divisional, regional, or departmental level
  * provide insight on specific laws, rules, regulations, or guidance relative to the area in which they operate
  * provide a lower-level and more granular view of the critical business processes that need to be executed as well as the procedures that surround them
* **Representatives from Marketing, Branding, and Promotion**
  * tells us
    * whether or not blockchain will have an effect on how and what we communicate to our existing and potential customers
    * if blockchain will change how we are perceived in the marketplace
  * a key audience in helping us understand whether the technology changes the way we see and present ourselves in the market place

{% hint style="info" %}
Using blockchain often has a very direct and noticeable impact on customer experience

‚ï∞‚îà‚û§ therefore it's critical to have representatives from the marketing, branding, and promotional areas of the organization
{% endhint %}

* **Representatives from Sales and Business Development**
  * help us understand
    * whether or not blockchain will change the way we attract, acquire, and retain customers
    * whether blockchain changes the value proposition behind any of our current offerings and if so, what that change looks like
    * whether use of blockchain is going to allow us to potentially acquire new customers that we weren't able to gain previously
  * help us evaluate our plan for onboarding and retaining these new customers
