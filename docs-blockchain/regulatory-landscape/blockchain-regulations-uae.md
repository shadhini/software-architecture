## Blockchain Regulations: United Arab Emirates

{% hint style="info" %}
UAE has adopted and embraced blockchain to the **largest degree**.
{% endhint %}

* **recognition of `Digital Signatures`**
  * recognition of digital signatures extended to include those executed on blockchain
    * UAE Federal Law No.1 of 2006 on Electronic Commerce and Transactions
    * [https://tahseen.ae/media/2670/uae-2006-ecommerce-and-transactions-law-english.pdf](https://tahseen.ae/media/2670/uae-2006-ecommerce-and-transactions-law-english.pdf)
* **UAE Blockchain Strategy**  program
  * UAE launched its Blockchain Strategy in 2018
  * many **public functions** conducted **on blockchain**&#x20;
  * cities like Dubai, Abu Dhabi stand as a model for what blockchain usage and  civic scenarios might look like for the rest of us in the future
* **`Financial Services Regulations`**
  * frameworks for blockchain-based financial services developed by&#x20;
    * `DFSA`: Dubai Financial Services Authority&#x20;
