## Blockchain Regulations: Belarus

{% hint style="info" %}
First country to create **official regulatory framework** for the blockchain industry.
{% endhint %}

* A 2017 law focuses on the blockchain companies centered in the _**`Hi-Tech Park`** (**`HTP`**)_
  * Belarusian "_Silicon Valley_"
  * "**`Digital Economy Development Ordinance`**" has been in effect since March 2018
* according to the law, **HTP** has been designated as a **special sector**
  * special tax and legal status for blockchain and crypto businesses
* companies-residents of the HTP are **not restricted** in **issuing**, **storing** or **trading** **digital tokens**
*   members of HTP granted a **six-year tax break**

    * a 2018 revision to the law focused on the prevention of terrorism financing, money laundering, and other illegal activities
