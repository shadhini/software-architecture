## Blockchain Regulations: China





* **Blockchain Service Provider Regulations**
  * in January 2019, China introduced regulations that required **blockchain service providers to register** with the `Cyberspace Administration of China` (**`CAC`**)
  * Guidelines related to **user identification** and **content management** must be followed&#x20;
* **`ICO` Ban**
  * China banned **Initial Coin Offerings** (`ICOs`) in September 2017
  * considered illegal fundraising activities, ban restricts ability of companies to raise funds through token sales&#x20;
* **`Cyptocurrency Exchanges`** **Ban**
  * cryptocurrency exchanges banned in 2017
  * many local exchanges shut down or have moved their operations abroad
* **Internet Content Regulation**
  * blockchain platforms are also subject to China's strict internet content regulations
  * content that was deemed illegal or posed a threat to national secuirty could lear to censorship or penalties
