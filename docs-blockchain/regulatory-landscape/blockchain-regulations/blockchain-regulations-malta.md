## Blockchain Regulations: Malta

{% hint style="info" %}
Malta has been keen to embrace blockchain technology, even going as far as calling itself "**Blockchain Island**"
{% endhint %}

2 Blockchain-related laws

* **`MDIA Act`: Malta Digital Innovation Authority Act**
  * focuses on **certifying blockchain** and **establishing standards** for the industry
  * [https://legislation.mt/eli/cap/591/eng/pdf](https://legislation.mt/eli/cap/591/eng/pdf)
* **`ITAS Act`: Innovative Technology Arrangement and Services Act**
  * focuses on **setting up ledgers** and **regulating the entry of new** blockchains
  * [https://legislation.mt/eli/cap/592/eng/pdf](https://legislation.mt/eli/cap/592/eng/pdf)
