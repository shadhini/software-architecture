# AML/KYC Compliance

## `AML`/`KYC` Compliance

> **`AML`**: **Anti-Money Laundering**
>
> **`KYC`**: **Know Your Customer**

### Why they are significant

**1. Preventing Money Laundering:**

* AML regulations require businesses to detect and report suspicious activities
* prevent use of cryptocurrencies to obscure the origins of illegally obtained funds

**2. Combating Terrorist Financing:**

* cryptocurrencies provide an anonymous and borderless means of transferring funds.
* AML regulations mandate the monitoring transactions for suspicious links
* block funding to terrorist organizations&#x20;

**3. Enhancing Financial System Integrity:**

* money laundering or illicit activities in cryptocurrencies harm the integrity of the  financial system
* AML regulations bridge the gap between crypto and traditional finance
* Maintain system credibility and stability

**4. Protecting Investors:**

* the pseudonymous nature of cryptocurrencies makes it difficult to identify bad actors
* KYC regulations require cryptocurrency exchanges and service providers to verify user identities
* discourage scams and fraud

**5. Regulatory Compliance:**

* Meet local AML/KYC regulations
* Avoid legal and financial penalties
