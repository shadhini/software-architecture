# Digital Asset Custody

## Digital Asset Custody

> Digital assets&#x20;
>
> * cryptocurrencies
> * tokens&#x20;
> * other blockchain-based assets
>
> need to be **stored and managed safely** to protect them from&#x20;
>
> * theft,&#x20;
> * loss, or&#x20;
> * unauthorized access.



### Challenges of Secure Custody for Digital Assets and Their Implications&#x20;

**1. Regulatory Compliance:**

* Complex, global regulations&#x20;
  * AML/KYC requirements
* Risk:&#x20;
  * non-compliance: Legal penalties
  * compliance: limited services

**2. Security Risks:**

* Hacking & theft threats
* Fraud exposure
* Risk: Financial loss, trust erosion

**3. Institutional Custody:**

* Demand for institutional-grade solutions
* lack of institutional-grade custody solutions&#x20;
  * Barrier: Limited options for large investors
  * Risk: Market growth restricted

**4. Key Management:**

* Private key protection
* Risk: Irreversible asset loss
* Importance: User education

**5. User Experience:**

* Complexity vs. usability
  * balancing security & user-friendliness&#x20;
* Tech barriers for mainstream users
* Risk: Poor adoption, insecure practices

**6. Multisignature and Governance:**

* Multi-key (Multisignature) wallet complexity
* Governance and access disputes
* Risk: Locked/inaccessible funds

**7. Third-Party Risks:**

* Counterparty risk â€” introduced by delegating custody to third-party providers
* Provider trust issues
* Risk: Loss from provider failure or attack
