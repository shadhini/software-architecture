# Geopolitical Risk

## Geopolitical Risk

* **Regulatory Uncertainty**
  * different governments —  varying approaches to blockchain & crypto
  * inconsistent/evolving regulations
* **Bans and Restrictions**
  * cryptocurrency trading bans
  * `ICOs`: Initial Coin Offerings bans
* **Tax Evasion and Money Laundering**
  * governments' concern — blockchains' pseudonymous transactions — make it difficult for authorities to regulate transactions — tax evasion & money laundering
* **Censorship and Control**
  * blockchain's decentralized nature — challenges traditional government control&#x20;
* **Data Privacy and Sovereignty**
  * blockchain's transparency & immutability — clash with data privacy regulations&#x20;
  * governments' concerns&#x20;
    * sensitive information being exposed on a public ledger&#x20;
    * sensitive information stored outside their borders
* [**Sanctions**](#user-content-fn-1)[^1] **Evasion**
  * blockchain's pseudonymous transactions — used for evading economic sanctions or facilitating illicit activities
* **Jurisdictional Conflicts**
  * ﻿﻿cross-border transactions&#x20;
  * determining laws to be applied and which jurisdiction has authority --  complex
* **Legal Recognition of Smart Contracts**
  * ﻿﻿enforceability of smart contracts --  questioned by legal systems
* **Token Classification**
  * different legal classification of tokens in different jurisdictions
    * security tokens, utility tokens
  * affects regulatory requirements and obligations
* **Interoperability and International Standards**
  * lack of interoperability standards and international agreements&#x20;
    * fragmentation in blockchain applications&#x20;
    * hinder cross-border adoption
* **National Security Concerns**
  * governments concerns — use of blockchains' encrypted communication as means to bypass traditional surveillance methods
* **Technological Dependence**
  * relying heavily on technology from a foreign source —  leave a country vulnerable to supply chain disruptions or security risks





[^1]: a threatened penalty for disobeying a law or rule
