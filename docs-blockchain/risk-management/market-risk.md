# Market Risk

## Market Risk

— risks that exists as we bring our solution to market —&#x20;

* **Technological Risks**
  * ﻿﻿`Scalability` &#x20;
    * as number of transactions/participants increases — slower transaction speeds and higher costs
  * ﻿﻿`Interoperability`&#x20;
    * seamless communication between different blockchain networks and legacy systems — complex, may hinder adoption
  * ﻿﻿`Security`&#x20;
    * vulnerabilities in&#x20;
      * smart contracts
      * consensus mechanisms
      * cryptographic protocols &#x20;
* **Regulatory and Legal Risks**
  * ﻿﻿`Uncertain Regulations`&#x20;
    * evolving/inconsistent regulations across jurisdictions — impact blockchain adoption involving cryptocurrencies and token sales
  * ﻿﻿`Compliance`&#x20;
    * regulatory frameworks — AML, KYC
* **Partnerships and Collaboration Risks**
  * ﻿﻿`Integration Challenges`&#x20;
    * challenging and time consuming
      * collaborating with existing industry players &#x20;
      * integrating blockchain solutions with legacy systems&#x20;
  * ﻿﻿`Dependency`&#x20;
    * relying on others for key components
      * third-party vendors &#x20;
      * partners
* **Public Perception Risks**
  * ﻿﻿`Misunderstanding`&#x20;
    * misinformation — unrealistic expectations, skepticism about capabilities
  * ﻿﻿`Negative Association`&#x20;
    * perception influenced by its association with cryptocurrencies linked to illegal activities or speculative behavior
* **Environmental Concerns**
  * `Energy Consumption`&#x20;
    * Proof of Work consensus — energy-intensive — high carbon footprint
* **Investment Concerns**
  * ﻿﻿`Volatility`&#x20;
    * cryptocurrency related projects: high price volatility
  * ﻿﻿[`Speculative`](#user-content-fn-1)[^1] `Behavior`&#x20;
    * attract speculative investments — leading to market bubbles and crashes





[^1]: based on conjecture rather than knowledge
