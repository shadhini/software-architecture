# Consumer Applications vs Enterprise Applications

## Consumer Applications vs Enterprise Applications

<table><thead><tr><th width="139.04296875"></th><th valign="top">Consumer -- B2C</th><th valign="top">Enterprise -- B2B</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td></td><td valign="top"><strong><code>B2C</code></strong>: products or services designed for individual end users</td><td valign="top"><strong><code>B2B</code></strong>: products or services designed for companies/organizations</td></tr><tr><td>Focus</td><td valign="top">ease of use, low friction, price points, broad reach</td><td valign="top">scale, security, integrations, governance, contractual SLAs</td></tr><tr><td>Users &#x26; buyers</td><td valign="top">individual people choose and use the product themselves (download an app, subscribe)</td><td valign="top">purchasers are organizations (IT, procurement, department heads); end users may be many employees</td></tr><tr><td>Scale &#x26; deployment</td><td valign="top">single-user installs or cloud accounts; limited multi-user coordination</td><td valign="top">multi-user, multi-tenant or on-prem deployments, directory integration, centralized administration</td></tr><tr><td>Features and priorities</td><td valign="top">polished UX, discoverability, immediate value, social/sharing features</td><td valign="top">administrative controls, role-based access, audit logs, reporting, workflow automation</td></tr><tr><td>Security &#x26; compliance</td><td valign="top">standard protections (TLS, app permissions); lower emphasis on regulatory compliance</td><td valign="top">strong encryption, SSO/SAML/OAuth, data residency, compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC2), penetration testing, contract clauses</td></tr><tr><td>Reliability &#x26; SLAs</td><td valign="top">best-effort availability; informal support</td><td valign="top">formal SLAs, uptime guarantees, business-continuity planning, dedicated support channels</td></tr><tr><td>Integration &#x26; extensibility</td><td valign="top">limited integrations (social login, payment)</td><td valign="top">deep integrations with ERP/CRM/IDPs, APIs, SSO, custom connectors, bulk import/export</td></tr><tr><td>Customization &#x26; configurability</td><td valign="top">fixed experience or light personalization</td><td valign="top">heavy configuration, tenant-specific customizations, white-labeling, workflow tailoring</td></tr><tr><td>Pricing &#x26; procurement</td><td valign="top">low price or freemium, self-serve purchase through app stores</td><td valign="top">negotiated contracts, volume/license tiers, annual recurring revenue, purchase orders, multi-year deals</td></tr><tr><td>Support &#x26; onboarding</td><td valign="top">in-app help, community forums, email support</td><td valign="top">dedicated onboarding, training, account manager, professional services</td></tr><tr><td>Governance &#x26; control</td><td valign="top">individual control over account/data</td><td valign="top">centralized governance, auditability, policies, role delegation</td></tr><tr><td>Lifecycle &#x26; upgrade path</td><td valign="top">frequent small updates pushed automatically</td><td valign="top">controlled upgrade windows, change management, staging and rollback processes</td></tr><tr><td>Metrics &#x26; success criteria</td><td valign="top">downloads, DAU/MAU, retention, conversion, CAC</td><td valign="top">time-to-value, ROI, user adoption across departments, churn by contract</td></tr><tr><td>Examples</td><td valign="top"><code>Spotify</code>, <code>Instagram</code>, <code>TikTok</code>, personal budgeting apps</td><td valign="top"><code>Salesforce</code>, <code>Workday</code>, <code>Okta</code>, <code>SAP</code>, enterprise backup solutions</td></tr></tbody></table>
